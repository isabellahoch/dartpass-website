What is DartPass?

DartPass is the first all-in-one digital substitute for the physical Dartmouth ID card. It is a mobile app designed to allow users the same benefits as the traditional DartCard students pick up during Orientation week, and more.

Acess Buildings

Let's face it, Hanover winters are hard. They're even harder when you're locked out of your dorm building at 2 AM because you don't have your ID. Soon, you'll be able to access buildings with just your phone!

Pay for Meals

Ever had to miss a meal or be late to meet a friend at Foco because you forgot your ID? These worries are past troubles with DartPass; all you need to swipe in is your phone!

Transfer DBA*

Do you wish you could "Venmo" a friend DBA to pick you up a Collis muffin? With DartPass, you'll be able to integrate with your dining plan, delegate meal swipes, and more.

* Pending Administration Approval

Mobile Wallet Integration

DartPass will employ HID Global Mobile Wallet Authentication Technology to let you pay for your Collis pasta with Apple Pay!

  • Secure – HID Global market-leading security and convenience
  • Flexible – Use with cards and wearables
  • Future proof – Leverage one ID for various uses – shortening time to ROI and reducing total cost of ownership
  • Easily integrated – Fits into the Apple ecosystem and Apple Wallet without modification
